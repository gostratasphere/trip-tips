Trip Tips

We remind you before you forget.

We send you useful tips for your travel 1 week and 1 day ahead of your trip to minimize those “oh fml” moments we all hate so much, like getting to a conference without your business cards, or realizing after you got to the airport that your airline required ID for your infant.

How does it work?

It’s easy: you book your trip, and Trip Tips automagically sends you tips just by having your trip on your Google Calendar.

No matter where your trips take you, Trip Tips are along for the ride.
